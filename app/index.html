About me

Hi, I'm Valeria. First and foremost, I’m the mother of two lovely girls, who have been the inspiration behind my life’s transformation. My children reignited my sense of childhood wonder, where imagination has no limits. I started by organizing small birthday parties for them, which quickly grew into a passion for creating events. This led to the launch of Magic Art Studio, where I collaborated with teachers and artists to offer sand animation, art, and craft lessons for kids. We also hosted themed birthday parties, such as Princess, Pirate, Detective, Harry Potter, and more.
Over time, my business evolved into a mobile entertainment company. Now, we bring joy directly to families and audiences through magical performances, and I can't imagine my life without the happiness I create at every event.

Questions and Answers

  • How long is the Magic Bubble Show?
    For kids aged 5 years and older, the show typically lasts 45–60 minutes, including the exciting moment of putting kids inside a huge soap bubble at the end of the performance. For younger children, the program is shorter — up to 45 minutes — since they may find it challenging to concentrate for extended periods.
  • Where is the best location for the Magic Bubble Show?
    Please remember this is indoor performance!

    Any indoor area is suitable: a home, playroom, indoor playground, or community hall. We create a performance area that is 9 feet wide and 6 feet deep to separate myself from the audience. Kids will sit on chairs, other furnishings, or on the floor around my setup.

    However, there are a few conditions required:
    – If you are hosting the party at home, please organize an open space free of toys, other items, and electronic devices. Remember, it is your responsibility to provide a clear space for the performance.
    – If there is an air conditioner or fan in the performance area, it needs to be turned off temporarily.
    – Eating and drinking during the performance is prohibited, and bringing food into the performance area (including popcorn and chips) is not allowed.
  • Will my floor get wet during The Bubble Show?
    We bring special waterproof carpets to protect your floor, and I ensure that all bubbles are made and popped within the protected area.
    Our bubble solution contains no organic ingredients that could grow bacteria, cause odors, or leave stains. We also clean the area after the performance.
    If you have items that need protection near the performance area (such as electronic devices or delicate furniture), please remove them or cover them with cloth.
  • Can The Magic Bubble Show be presented outdoors?
    The Magic Bubble Show must be performed indoors, as it includes many bubble tricks that cannot be done outside.
    Placing people inside a giant soap bubble is almost not possible outdoors due to wind or breezes.
    For outdoor events, we offer our Outside Bubble Play service instead.
  • Will the Magic Bubble Show be interesting for adults?
    Yes, absolutely! The show is designed to captivate audiences of all ages. For adult events, I offer a special program with appropriate music tailored to the occasion, whether it's a wedding, baby shower, or any other celebration. The Magic Mirror service is also popular for adult events.
  • How much time do you need for set up?
    We arrive at least 30 minutes before the performance to set up.

Policies & payment details

Travel within the West Edmonton area is included. An additional transportation fee applies if your location is outside the west side of Edmonton. Please provide your address to check if an extra fee is required.
All prices are subject to 5% GST.

A deposit is required upon booking, with the remaining amount due on the day of the party. The deposit is non-refundable and will not be returned if the event is canceled by the client less than 2 weeks in advance. In case of illness or emergency, we will do our best to accommodate your booking at another, more convenient time (within 60 days of your original party date).

Please note that we assume no responsibility or liability for any accidents or damages caused during your party to guests or property. We are not responsible for the supervision of children; adult presence is required at all times. While your party hostess/entertainer will take care to ensure your home and guests are treated with respect, liability remains solely with the client.
