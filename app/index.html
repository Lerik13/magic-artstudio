About me

I am Valeria Kan, first of all I am mother of two lovely girls. They are the main reason of all my life transformation. My kids bring me back into the brightness child feeling without any borders for fantasy and imagination. I started from organizing small birthday parties for my girls, than for friends and so forth. And now I can't live without enjoyment seeing happiness of everyone, who involved in events, which I with my team are making in my Magic Art Studio.
