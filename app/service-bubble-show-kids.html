Bubble adventure for everyone!

Are you dreaming of giving your child the Best Birthday Ever? Look no further! We are here to make this day truly special and memorable.

Questions and Answers

  • How long is the Magic Bubble Show?
    For kids aged 5 years and older, the show typically lasts 45â€“60 minutes, including the exciting moment of putting kids inside a huge soap bubble at the end of the performance. For younger children, the program is shorter — up to 45 minutes — since they may find it challenging to concentrate for extended periods.
  • Where is the best location for the Magic Bubble Show?
    Please remember this is indoor performance!

    Any indoor area is suitable: a home, playroom, indoor playground, or community hall. We create a performance area that is 9 feet wide and 6 feet deep to separate myself from the audience. Kids will sit on chairs, other furnishings, or on the floor around my setup.

    However, there are a few conditions required:
    – If you are hosting the party at home, please organize an open space free of toys, other items, and electronic devices. Remember, it is your responsibility to provide a clear space for the performance.
    – If there is an air conditioner or fan in the performance area, it needs to be turned off temporarily.
    – Eating and drinking during the performance is prohibited, and bringing food into the performance area (including popcorn and chips) is not allowed.
  • Will my floor get wet during The Bubble Show?
    We bring special waterproof carpets to protect your floor, and I ensure that all bubbles are made and popped within the protected area.
    Our bubble solution contains no organic ingredients that could grow bacteria, cause odors, or leave stains. We also clean the area after the performance.
    If you have items that need protection near the performance area (such as electronic devices or delicate furniture), please remove them or cover them with cloth.
  • Can The Magic Bubble Show be presented outdoors?
    The Magic Bubble Show must be performed indoors, as it includes many bubble tricks that cannot be done outside.
    Placing people inside a giant soap bubble is almost not possible outdoors due to wind or breezes.
    For outdoor events, we offer our Outside Bubble Play service instead.
  • Will the Magic Bubble Show be interesting for adults?
    Yes, absolutely! The show is designed to captivate audiences of all ages. For adult events, I offer a special program with appropriate music tailored to the occasion, whether it's a wedding, baby shower, or any other celebration. The Magic Mirror service is also popular for adult events.
  • How much time do you need for set up?
    We arrive at least 30 minutes before the performance to set up.
