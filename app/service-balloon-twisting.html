Bubble adventure for everyone!

Balloon twisting is the ideal enhancement for any event! A vibrant and talented balloon artist will visit your home or selected venue to craft stunning balloon hats, animals, and cartoon characters for your child and guests. Plus, as a delightful bonus, every guest will receive a bubble cake at the end of the party!

Included in the show:
  • Wild balloon shapes
  • Bright balloon colors
  • Balloon for each child
  • Custom balloon hats
  • Custom balloon animals
  • Custom balloon swords
  • Birthday balloon blast

The price for Balloon Twisting is $200 for up to 10 kids ($10 for each additional child)
For holiday events and larger audiences, please contact us for pricing.

Travel within the West Edmonton area is included. An extra transportation fee applies if your location is outside the west side of Edmonton.
All prices are subject to 5% GST.

Policies & payment details

Travel within the West Edmonton area is included. An additional transportation fee applies if your location is outside the west side of Edmonton. Please provide your address to check if an extra fee is required.
All prices are subject to 5% GST.

A deposit is required upon booking, with the remaining amount due on the day of the party. The deposit is non-refundable and will not be returned if the event is canceled by the client less than 2 weeks in advance. In case of illness or emergency, we will do our best to accommodate your booking at another, more convenient time (within 60 days of your original party date).

Please note that we assume no responsibility or liability for any accidents or damages caused during your party to guests or property. We are not responsible for the supervision of children; adult presence is required at all times. While your party hostess/entertainer will take care to ensure your home and guests are treated with respect, liability remains solely with the client.
